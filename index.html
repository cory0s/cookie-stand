About Us

Seattle's Best Salmon Cookies was founded by a few people who just really love salmon. It all started in our basement in Alaska when a few of us dreamed what could be if the worlds of fine cookies and fresh Alaskan salmon were meshed. We think our results are delicious and we hope you do too.
